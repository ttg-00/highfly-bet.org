What are cookies?

Cookies represent small text files that websites store on your computer, smartphone, or tablet when you visit them. These digital markers contain information about your browsing behavior, preferences, and interaction patterns with specific websites. Highflybet utilizes various cookie categories to enhance user experience, maintain security protocols, and deliver relevant content recommendations.

Think of cookies as digital bookmarks that help websites remember your previous visits and personalized settings. They enable features like keeping you logged in, remembering your language preferences, and maintaining shopping cart contents. Highfly bet ensures all cookies comply with international privacy standards and industry best practices. Modern browsers provide built-in tools for managing cookie preferences, giving users complete control over their digital footprint.
